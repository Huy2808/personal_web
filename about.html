NGUYEN Phu-Huy

Master's Student in Astrophysics and Space Science

I am Huy, and I am from Hanoi, Vietnam. Currently, I am an Erasmus Mundus master student in Astrophysics and Space Science at the University of Rome "Tor Vergata".

The questions that keep me up at night are: How did the Universe begin? What is the nature of dark matter and dark energy? How do the grand structures form from the primordial fluctuations? I am passionate about exploring these questions through both simulations and observation.

You can find more about my academic journey in my CV, my current and pass research in Research, and my study notes in Notes. I also love to travel and explore new places, which you can see in my Travel, which are the list of events and places I have visited and given talks.
