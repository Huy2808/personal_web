Mathematical Methods for Physics

Last updated: October 2025 Mathematics

Introduction

Just math... but the kind that makes physics possible.

Vector Calculus

Gradient, Divergence, and Curl

Gradient:

$$\nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\hat{x} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\hat{y} + \frac{\partial f}{\partial z}\hat{z}$$

Divergence:

$$\nabla \cdot \vec{F} = \frac{\partial F_x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial F_y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial F_z}{\partial z}$$

Curl:

$$\nabla \times \vec{F} = \begin{vmatrix} \hat{x} & \hat{y} & \hat{z} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\ F_x & F_y & F_z \end{vmatrix}$$

Important Theorems

Divergence Theorem:

$$\iiint_V (\nabla \cdot \vec{F}) \, dV = \iint_S \vec{F} \cdot d\vec{S}$$

Stokes' Theorem:

$$\iint_S (\nabla \times \vec{F}) \cdot d\vec{S} = \oint_C \vec{F} \cdot d\vec{l}$$

Differential Equations

Ordinary Differential Equations

First Order Linear:

$$\frac{dy}{dx} + P(x)y = Q(x)$$

Solution:

$$y = e^{-\int P dx}\left(\int Q e^{\int P dx} dx + C\right)$$

Second Order Homogeneous:

$$ay'' + by' + cy = 0$$

Characteristic equation: $ar^2 + br + c = 0$

Partial Differential Equations

Wave Equation:

$$\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial t^2} = c^2 \nabla^2 u$$

Heat Equation:

$$\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = \alpha \nabla^2 u$$

Laplace Equation:

$$\nabla^2 u = 0$$

Complex Analysis

Euler's Formula

$$e^{i\theta} = \cos\theta + i\sin\theta$$

Cauchy-Riemann Equations

$$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}, \quad \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = -\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}$$

Residue Theorem

$$\oint_C f(z) dz = 2\pi i \sum \text{Res}(f, z_k)$$

Fourier Analysis

Fourier Series

$$f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left(a_n \cos(nx) + b_n \sin(nx)\right)$$

where:

$$a_n = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x)\cos(nx) dx$$ $$b_n = \frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x)\sin(nx) dx$$

Fourier Transform

$$\tilde{f}(k) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x) e^{-ikx} dx$$

Inverse Transform:

$$f(x) = \frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \tilde{f}(k) e^{ikx} dk$$

Special Functions

Bessel Functions

Solutions to Bessel's equation:

$$x^2\frac{d^2y}{dx^2} + x\frac{dy}{dx} + (x^2 - n^2)y = 0$$

Legendre Polynomials

Rodrigues formula:

$$P_n(x) = \frac{1}{2^n n!}\frac{d^n}{dx^n}(x^2-1)^n$$

Spherical Harmonics

$$Y_l^m(\theta,\phi) = \sqrt{\frac{(2l+1)(l-m)!}{4\pi(l+m)!}}P_l^m(\cos\theta)e^{im\phi}$$

Linear Algebra

Eigenvalue Problem

$$A\vec{v} = \lambda\vec{v}$$

Characteristic equation:

$$\det(A - \lambda I) = 0$$

Matrix Diagonalization

$$A = PDP^{-1}$$

Tensor Analysis

Metric Tensor

$$ds^2 = g_{\mu\nu}dx^\mu dx^\nu$$

Christoffel Symbols

$$\Gamma^\lambda_{\mu\nu} = \frac{1}{2}g^{\lambda\sigma}\left(\frac{\partial g_{\sigma\mu}}{\partial x^\nu} + \frac{\partial g_{\sigma\nu}}{\partial x^\mu} - \frac{\partial g_{\mu\nu}}{\partial x^\sigma}\right)$$

Green's Functions

Solution to inhomogeneous differential equation:

$$\mathcal{L}G(x,x') = \delta(x-x')$$

General solution:

$$u(x) = \int G(x,x')f(x') dx'$$

Variational Calculus

Euler-Lagrange Equation

$$\frac{\partial L}{\partial q} - \frac{d}{dt}\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{q}} = 0$$

Applications in Physics

  • Quantum Mechanics: Schrödinger equation, spherical harmonics
  • Electromagnetism: Maxwell's equations, vector calculus
  • General Relativity: Tensor analysis, differential geometry
  • Statistical Mechanics: Fourier analysis, special functions
